About Atrovia

Most owners don't fail because they lack tools. They fail because nobody hands them a plan, sets it up the right way, and stays to help them execute. Atrovia was built to fix exactly that.

Our story

Two powerful programs, combined into one.

Atrovia didn't start as one product. It started as two — each solving a different half of the same problem.

Atrium was built to handle growth: brand, marketing, a real 90-day plan, and a steady flow of new customers. Kova was built to handle the customers you already have: a CRM that's actually set up right, automated follow-up, and repeat business on autopilot.

Owners kept telling us the same thing — getting customers and keeping customers can't live in two disconnected places. So we combined them into one guided platform, with a real team walking you through every step. Together, they're Atrovia — a small business growth platform.

Meet the founders

Built by people who've actually done it.

Austin and Michael met at a networking event and connected right away — one building small businesses, the other bringing decades of deep technical firepower. As they started working together, each was building a separate program. They blended them into Atrovia.

AR
Austin Reinders
Co-founder · President & CEO

Austin has spent his career building small businesses from the ground up. Over the last six years he went deep on the technology side — applications, marketing, workflows, and how to actually incorporate tech into a small business.

That work became Atrium, the software he first built to grow his own companies — the marketing and growth engine that now powers Atrovia.

MR
Michael Rabot
Co-founder · COO

Michael has spent 30+ years in Silicon Valley across IT and a range of industries, with deep expertise in complex server environments, security, and backend and frontend application development.

He brings the technical backbone — the engineering and security foundation that makes Atrovia powerful enough to run a real business on.

When the two came together, they blended their programs into one platform and built what they believe is the ultimate small business platform — powerful technology, made genuinely simple to adopt.
